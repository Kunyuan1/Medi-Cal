Services We Offer

Here are some of the services we provide.

Scheduler

Our scheduling feature is designed to streamline the complex process of managing surgical appointments. Surgeons can effortlessly schedule and update their surgeries, appointments, and availability through an intuitive interface. It ensures that surgeons and their teams stay informed and organized. By reducing administrative burdens, our scheduling feature allows healthcare professionals to focus more on patient care and less on logistics, ultimately enhancing the efficiency and effectiveness of surgical operations.

Timetable

Our timetable feature offers a comprehensive and user-friendly solution for managing surgical schedules. Surgeons can view and organize their daily or weekly timetables with ease, ensuring a clear overview of their commitments. Real-time synchronization ensures that any updates are immediately reflected across the entire timetable. With advanced filtering options, surgeons can customize their view to focus on specific time periods or activities.

Follow-up Checkups

Our follow-up checkup feature ensures that post-operative care is efficiently managed and never overlooked. Surgeons can easily schedule and track follow-up appointments for their patients to ensure timely visits. The feature allows for comprehensive note-taking and documentation, enabling surgeons to monitor patient recovery and progress closely. This feature helps maintain continuity of care, improves patient outcomes, and fosters better communication between surgeons and their patients.
