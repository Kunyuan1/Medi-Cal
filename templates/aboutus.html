About Us

We are a group of motivated students wishing to make a difference

We are a group of motivated computer science students dedicated to making a difference in the medical field. Our current project involves developing an innovative scheduling web app specifically designed for surgeons. Our goal is to streamline the scheduling process, reduce administrative burdens, and ultimately improve the efficiency of surgical operations. By leveraging our technical skills and passion for problem-solving, we aim to create a tool that significantly enhances the workflow for healthcare professionals, leading to better patient care and optimized surgical procedures.
